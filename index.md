# Privacy Policy for AutoGuide

Effective date: 2026-01-15

AutoGuide ("the app") respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect
The app may access:
- Microphone (for voice narration and translation)
- Location (to provide nearby points of interest)

No audio recordings are stored or transmitted.

## How We Use Information
- Audio input is used only in real-time for narration and translation.
- Location data is used to show nearby points of interest.

## Data Storage
- No raw audio is stored.
- No personal data is sold or shared.

## Third-Party Services
The app may use third-party services (e.g. text-to-speech or maps) that process data according to their own privacy policies.

## User Rights
You can revoke permissions at any time via device settings.
